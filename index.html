About

Where governance meets nature-based innovation

M.Sc. Urban Management — Erasmus B.Arch (Honors) — Technion NBI Academy 2025 ESG Certified C40 & ICLEI

I'm a spatial planning and climate adaptation professional who has spent two decades translating complex urban challenges into implementable strategies for cities. My work sits at the intersection of environmental policy, data-driven decision-making, and cross-stakeholder governance.

As former Head of Environmental & Sustainable Planning at Haifa Municipality, I established a new department from the ground up, led the city's first Climate Adaptation Action Plan, and introduced GIS-based workflows for climate risk mapping and spatial prioritization.

Now based in the Netherlands, I bring a unique blend: the strategic vision to define what needs measuring and why, the governance fluency to navigate public sector procurement, and the implementation drive to push strategies from paper to the ground.

I don't just write plans — I build the methodology, define the KPIs, align the stakeholders, and drive implementation.
